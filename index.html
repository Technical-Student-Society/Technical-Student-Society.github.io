Learn

The Society has a major focus on education and learning. We run workshops to assist students with their studies and host other types of educational events such as talks and guest lectures that help in enchancingtheir skills.

As students, we are doing the best we can with our studies and learn as much as we can to develop our skills.

Socialise

We have over 100+ members in TSS#. All the volunteers and members are categorized into various teams.

Come along, bring your friends and meet other students and people sharing same interests as you.

Achievements

We undertake various projects, organize competitions where students showcase their skills and win amazing prizes.

We work with Technical, Sports and many other teams to successfully carry out and perform various tasks at TSS#

Thinking of joining?

New members are always welcome! As a member some things you will have access to include computer science talks and workshops, catered social events, a wide network of community to learn from and make friends with.

First year students

If you are a first year student there are many activities held by the club at the start of the year specifically for you. They will give you a chance to meet other students, help you ease into uni life and give you a head start with your subjects.
