About

About Us

Technical Student Society# is the only student society running in the Campus. The main motive of Society is to provide a good environment to the students so that they can enhance their Technical, Management and Communication Skills. The Society is Registered Society in Batala. It was established in August 2009 with the joint efforts of teachers and students. We welcome anyone interested in computer science and/or socialising to join!

Members will have access to:
  • Frequent computer science talks on a diverse range of topics
  • Educational workshops
  • Socialising with like minded society members
  • A wide network of other computer science students and graduates.
  • We also organize some other mini events and Sports Meet along with TechFest as our flagship event every year.

The society runs various competitions throughout the year offering members a chance to have fun and win prizes, these have included programming and video game competitions. The club also coordinates projects among club members, allowing members to gain valuable experience working on team projects - while also having fun.

Founded in 2009 with a large and active group of members, we are always willing to offer academic assistance or make new friends and all are welcome to join us for an exciting year in a fun, open and unique environment.

TSS# Slack

Technical Student Society#'s Slack server is a friendly place to chat with current and past members.

Please keep these points in mind when in the server:
  • Do not spam or flood the Slack with messages
  • Please be civil. Discussion is fine, but vitriolic behaviour is not
  • We do not condone any threats, humiliation, harassment or bullying towards another member
  • Please mark NSFW posts as so

Failure to follow these rules may result in being kicked or banned from the server.
